Synthwave sits between 90 and 130 BPM with moderate to high energy, built entirely on synthesizers, arpeggiated basslines, gated reverb drums, and a production aesthetic that deliberately evokes 1980s electronic music. The genre carries a strong sense of nostalgia and cinematic mood, often feeling like the soundtrack to a night drive or a retro science fiction film. Common moods include moody, brooding, energetic, and nostalgic, making synthwave a strong fit for focused late-night work, gaming, creative sessions, and driving at night. It appeals to listeners who want something with rhythmic drive but more atmosphere than traditional dance music. Synthwave overlaps with pop in its melodic hooks, with ambient music in its textural layers, and with r&b in its smooth, stylized production.
