Pop music typically runs between 100 and 130 BPM with moderate to high energy, built around catchy melodies, synthesizers, and polished production. Vocals are central and the arrangements are designed to feel immediately accessible, often leaning on verse-chorus structures with a strong hook. Common moods include happy, upbeat, and energetic, making pop a natural fit for parties, commutes, casual listening, and workouts. The genre serves a wide listener base and does not require a specific taste profile to enjoy. Pop overlaps significantly with indie pop in production style, with r&b in vocal technique, and with dance and electronic music in its use of synthesizers and rhythmic drive.
