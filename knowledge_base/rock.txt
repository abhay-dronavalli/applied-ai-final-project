Rock music typically ranges from 100 to 160 BPM with high energy, driven by electric guitars, bass, and live drums in a format that emphasizes power and forward momentum. The instrumentation is built for physical impact, with distorted guitars and loud dynamics that create a sense of urgency or release. Common moods include intense, energetic, and aggressive, making rock a strong choice for workouts, driving fast, or any situation that calls for high arousal. Rock listeners often want something that matches or amplifies their physical state rather than calming it. The genre overlaps with hip hop in raw energy, with indie pop in its guitar-forward arrangements, and with synthwave in its taste for loud, driving rhythms.
